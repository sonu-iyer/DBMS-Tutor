Types Of DBMS

  • Relational DBMS (RDBMS):

    • Organizes data into tables. Example: MySQL, Oracle.

  • NoSQL DBMS:

    • Handles unstructured data. Example: MongoDB, Redis.

  • Object-Oriented DBMS:

    • Integrates with OOP. Example: ObjectDB.

  • Hierarchical DBMS:

    • Data in tree-like structures. Example: IMS by IBM.
